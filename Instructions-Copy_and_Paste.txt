This is a simple version of the instructions to simplify Copy and Paste to 5250.  
It is adivasable to use this alongside the Intrucstions PDF.

Recommended to use RITFORI as USRPRF
CRTUSRPRF USRPRF(USRPRF) PASSWORD(password) CURLIB(RITFORI) SPCAUT(*ALLOBJ *SECADM *AUDIT) HOMEDIR('/usrprf') 

5250 Sign on as above USRPRF
MKDIR DIR('/tmp/ritfori') 
CD DIR('/tmp/ritfori')  
CALL PGM(QP2TERM)
wget -qO .curl.pem http://curl.haxx.se/ca/cacert.pem -no-check-certificate
wget --ca-certificate=.curl.pem -O https://github.com/RitFori/RitFori/archive/refs/heads/main.zip
unzip ./RitFori-main.zip
mv RitFori-main ritfori
F3

CRTBNDCL PGM(QTEMP/AcmeSetup1) SRCSTMF('/tmp/ritfori/code/AcmeSetup1.clle') DBGVIEW(*SOURCE)
CALL PGM(ACMESETUP1)

5250 Sign off and on again as above USRPRF

CALL PGM(ACMESETUP1) ACMESETUP2 EMAIL('e-mail') CERTNAME('name.domain.TLD') TOKEN('"domain_token"') JKSPASS('jks_password')

DCM Setup part 1 – Manual
DCM Setup part 2 – Manual
DCM Setup part 3

DCM_SETUP3 CERTNAME('name.domain.TLD') USRPWD('usr_password') DCMPWD('DCM_password') CERTAPP('certificate_app_name') 

Renew the certificate before 90 days

LERenew CERTNAME('name.domain.TLD') DCM_YN(YES) 

CALL PGM(LE_ADMIN5) 
DCMDltCert CERTNAME('name.domain.TLD')
